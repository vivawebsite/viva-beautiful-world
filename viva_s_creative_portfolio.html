ON THE COURT JERSEY #11

Finding My Fire, One Dribble at a Time

For me, basketball is where I practice courage! Being interested in sports builds my physical strength and team spirit, which is super important because my dream is to serve our nation as an army, navy, or airforce officer one day.

"Whenever I hold the ball, my mind clears. Every assist teaches me leadership, which will help me serve India in the defense forces!"

Dribbling, running past defense, and learning to make quick strategic plays teaches me discipline. The court isn't just a place to play; it is my training ground to become brave, robust, and supportive of my teammates!

ON THE STAGE KATHAK STORIES

The Grace of Ghungroos & Rhythms

If basketball is my fire, Kathak is my grace and concentration. The moment I tie the hundreds of brass bells (*Ghungroos*) around my ankles, I feel completely connected to India's beautiful cultural history.

"The fast tatkar footwork keeps me focused, while the rapid spinning chakkars make me feel like I am preparing for flight!"

Through hand gestures (*Mudras*) and rhythmic footwork, I get to express complex traditional Indian stories. Just like sketching or military discipline, Kathak requires hundreds of hours of patient practice to make the movements smooth and graceful. It teaches me deep mental strength.
